# Interface: Editor

The Editor is the heart of RhythmNator. This is where you will spend most of your time mapping your audio files.

![Full Interface](/images/editor/Editor.png)

---

## 1. Top Bar

The top bar groups project information and global actions.

![Top Bar](/images/editor/TopBar.png)

- **On the left**: Displays the tool title (**RhythmNator**) and the project tool name just below it.
- **On the right**: A series of icons for quick actions:
    - **Home**: Return to the project selection screen.
    - **Folder**: Open the folder containing the project files.
    - **Save**: Save your changes.
    - **JSON Export**: Export the final sheet in JSON format.
    - **Undo / Redo**: Undo or redo the last actions.
    - **Sidebar**: Open or close the left sidebar.

---

## 2. Work Area (Track)

The work area is divided into two main sections:

![Visualizer and Tracks](/images/editor/WaveForm.png)
![Track List](/images/editor/TrackListe.png)

1. **The Visualizer**: Allows you to see the music's waveform for visual precision when placing notes.
2. **The Track List**: Displays the different tracks created with the notes placed inside them.
3. **Playhead Line**: A vertical line that precisely indicates where you are in the music.

> To learn how to create and manage your tracks, consult the [Guide: Work Area (Track)](./guide-sheet.md).

---

## 3. Audio Panel

This panel manages everything related to the sound file and the basic rhythm.

![Audio Panel](/images/editor/AudioPanel.png)

- **Loading**: Area to load or change the audio file.
- **BPM**: Displays the current tempo. Click the **Lightning** icon to automatically detect the BPM.
- **Starting Offset**: Adjust the initial synchronization of the music.
- **Volume**: Control the playback volume in the editor.
- **Playback Speed**: Change the speed (e.g., 0.5x, 1x, 2x) to map difficult passages.
- **Mouse Indicator**: Shows or hides your cursor's position on the grid.

---

## 4. Tracks Panel

Manage your sheet's structure here.

![Tracks Panel](/images/editor/PistePanel.png)

- **New Track**: Create an additional track.
- **Edit**: Allows you to create or delete notes manually.
- **Selection**: Allows you to select multiple tracks or notes to move, delete, copy, or paste them.
- **Group Manager**: Organize your tracks by groups (e.g., Drums, Guitar).
- **Action Markers**: Manage specific events at certain points in the music.

---

## 5. The Player (Playback)

The player gives you total control over listening to your creation.

![Playback Controls](/images/editor/Playback.png)

- **Play / Pause**: Start or stop the audio.
- **Stop**: Stop playback and return to the beginning.
- **Navigation**: Use the buttons to fast-forward or rewind quickly through the music.
