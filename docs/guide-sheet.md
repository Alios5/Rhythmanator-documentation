# Guide: Work Area (Track)

Learn how to manage tracks and organize your sheet in the "Sheet".

## Creating a Track

1.  Go to the **Track Management Panel**.
2.  Click the **Create a New Track** button.
3.  A dialog will appear:
    -   Enter the **Name** of the new track.
    -   Choose the **Key** (keyboard) that will be used to place notes.
    -   Choose the **Color** of the notes for this track.

## Track Options

At the top left of each track is a **Label**. Click it to open the options menu:

-   **Edit**: Allows you to change settings (name, key, color).
-   **Hide Track**: Hides the track to declutter the view.
-   **Delete Track**: Deletes the track and its notes.

*(Group assignment and note manipulation are covered in their respective guides: [Guide: Groups](./guide-groups.md) and [Guide: Note](./guide-event-keys.md))*

## Navigation

Master movements in your project to edit with precision.

### The Player (Playback)

Located at the bottom of the screen, the player displays the total duration and current position.

-   **Play / Pause**: Starts or stops the music.
-   **Stop**: Stops and returns to the beginning or the last starting point.
-   **Skip +/- 10s**: Quickly advances or retreats by 10 seconds.
-   **Playback Bar**: Drag the slider to reach a specific moment.

### Visualizer and Work Area

-   **Click on Visualizer**: Click anywhere on the waveform (top) to instantly place the playhead there.
-   **Playhead Line**: The vertical line indicates your exact position in the music.
-   **Scrollbars**: Use horizontal and vertical bars to visually navigate the tracks area.

### Auto-Follow

The button on the far left of the player enables **Auto-Follow** mode.

-   **Enabled**: The view automatically scrolls to keep the playhead line visible.
-   **Disabled**: The playhead line can go off-screen, allowing you to work on another section during playback.
